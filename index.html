Compassionate Nursing Care at Your Doorstep

MedMitram, meaning "Medical Friend," is committed to providing high-quality, personalized nursing services in the comfort of your home. Our trained professionals ensure that every patient receives the attention, care, and respect they deserve, while relieving families from the stress of hospital visits for routine procedures.

About MedMitram

We understand that recovery and medical care can be stressful, especially when it involves traveling to healthcare facilities for simple but essential procedures. MedMitram was founded with the vision to bring hospital-level care to your home, ensuring comfort, safety, and convenience. From post-surgery care to chronic illness management, our mission is to provide reliable and compassionate services that improve the quality of life for our patients and their families.

Our Services

Patient Care

We provide 24/7 or part-time nursing support, ensuring that your loved one is always in safe hands. Our caregivers assist with mobility, hygiene, medication reminders, and emotional support.

Injection & Cannulization

Our skilled nurses perform injections and IV cannulization at home, maintaining strict hygiene and minimizing discomfort for patients who require frequent administration.

Urinary Catheterization

Professional insertion, care, and replacement of urinary catheters, with patient education on maintenance to prevent infections and complications.

Ryles Tube & Tracheostomy Care

Careful handling, cleaning, and monitoring of Ryles tube and tracheostomy to ensure patient safety and comfort during recovery or chronic care.
